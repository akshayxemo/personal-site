Pulse gives product teams a live view of how users move through their apps — funnels, retention, and event streams that update without a refresh.

## The challenge

Analytics dashboards live or die on latency. Teams abandon tools that make them wait. The goal was sub-second interactions over a dataset measured in billions of rows.

## How it works

We split storage by access pattern: transactional metadata in PostgreSQL, and a columnar store (ClickHouse) for the analytical queries.

```sql title="funnel.sql"
SELECT
  step,
  count(DISTINCT user_id) AS users
FROM events
WHERE event_name IN ('view', 'signup', 'activate')
GROUP BY step
ORDER BY step;
```

Live tiles subscribe over WebSockets and patch only the deltas, so a busy dashboard never re-fetches its full payload.

<Callout>
  The biggest win came from pre-aggregating common time buckets. Most dashboards ask the same handful of questions — caching those shapes turned multi-second scans into instant reads.
</Callout>

## Impact

- p95 query latency under **400ms** on production workloads.
- Reduced infrastructure cost per query by **60%** versus the legacy pipeline.
- Powers daily decisions for product, growth, and support teams.
