## Overview

Creativenuances is a production-ready fashion e-commerce platform built for a real client, designed to deliver a seamless shopping experience while providing administrators with powerful content and inventory management capabilities.

The platform integrates a modern storefront, CMS-driven content management, secure payment processing, inventory tracking, order management, customer reviews, promotional campaigns, and shipping workflows.

**Status:** Live Production Application

---

## Key Features

### Customer Experience

* Responsive and mobile-friendly shopping experience
* Product discovery with advanced search and filtering
* Category and collection-based browsing
* Product reviews and ratings
* Wishlist and shopping cart management
* Coupon and discount support
* User account management
* Secure checkout flow

### Product Management

* CMS-driven product catalog management
* Inventory and stock tracking
* Dynamic banners and promotional offers
* Size chart management
* New arrivals and trending product sections

### Order & Commerce

* Razorpay payment gateway integration
* Order lifecycle management
* Customer address management
* Coupon validation and application
* Review and feedback collection

### Administrative Features

* Strapi CMS integration
* Content management for products, banners, FAQs, policies, and offers
* Inventory management workflows
* Customer and order monitoring
