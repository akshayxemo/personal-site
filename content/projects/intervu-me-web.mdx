## Overview

IntervuMe is a full-stack mentorship and interview preparation platform that connects job seekers with experienced mentors through live mock interview sessions.

The platform enables users to schedule interviews, participate in real-time video sessions, receive structured performance feedback, visualize improvement trends, communicate with mentors through instant messaging, and manage premium subscriptions through Stripe-powered billing.

### Live Mock Interviews

* Real-time 1-on-1 video interview sessions
* Mentor availability scheduling
* Session booking and management
* Interview status tracking

### Performance Evaluation System

* Mentor-driven interview assessments

* Rating-based evaluation framework
* Historical performance tracking
